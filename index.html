Research

I'm relatively new and interested in the field of applied machine learning research, specifically, computer vision, image processing, and generative AI. Recently, I've been exploring the use of generative AI for clinical applications. My first publication is listed below.

Medical Imaging Complexity and its Effects on GAN Performance
William Cagas*, Chan Ko, Blake Hsiao, Shryuk Grandhi, Rishi Bhattacharya, Michael Lam†, Kevin Zhu†

GAISynMeD @ ACCV, 2024 (Oral Presentation)

project page / paper / poster / slides

We show how increased complexity in medical image datasets leads to reduced GAN performance, and how this insight can guide efficient GAN training for medical image synthesis.
